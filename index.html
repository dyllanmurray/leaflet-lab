Background

Outside Magazine publishes a list of 25 cities and towns every year that the editors believe to be idealic for their subscribers. These cities and towns span all across the United States. These locations are great for people interested in craft beers, outside activities, and exceptional food!

Access to the outdoors is very important to the readers of Outside Magazine. A National Parks layer is available for those interested in learning which parks are closest to each of the Top 25 towns!
